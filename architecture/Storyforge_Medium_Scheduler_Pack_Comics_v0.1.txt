STORYFORGE MEDIUM SCHEDULER PACK — COMICS v0.1
Status: Canonical (Execution Support)
Scope: execution-support (comic-specific)

============================================================
1. PURPOSE
============================================================

Maps Emotional IR beats into comic panel slots.
Placement only. No story invention.

============================================================
2. COMIC PAGE GRID
============================================================

Default grid: 5–7 panels

Slots:
SLOT-1 OPENING
SLOT-2 CONTINUATION
SLOT-3 CONTINUATION
SLOT-H HINGE
SLOT-R REACTION
SLOT-E END-CAP

============================================================
3. BEAT → SLOT RULES
============================================================

INGRESS → SLOT-1 (+ SLOT-2 optional)
UT → SLOT-H only
COST → SLOT-H or SLOT-R
SMD → SLOT-R or SLOT-E
EXIT → SLOT-E

============================================================
4. HARD FAILURES
============================================================

- UT not isolated
- ingress shares conflict slot
- SMD absent by end

============================================================
END OF COMICS SCHEDULER
